Do I need both Launchpad and Nova OS?
Not necessarily. Launchpad is designed for pre-launch founders who are still building and validating. Nova OS is for businesses that are already live and need to scale their operations. That said, most clients use both — Launchpad to sharpen strategy, Nova OS to run the business automatically.
How long does Nova OS setup take?
Typically 14 days from your kickoff call. We handle everything — the only thing we need from you is a 60-minute onboarding session to understand your business, then we get to work.
Is Launchpad free to try?
Some tools have limited free access so you can see the quality of output before committing. Full access to all 10 tools requires a Launchpad plan. Check the pricing page for current options.
What if I'm already running a business?
Skip straight to Phase 2. Nova OS is for businesses at any stage — whether you're doing $10k/month or $100k/month, the same automation gaps exist. Book a call and we'll audit where you're losing time and revenue.
