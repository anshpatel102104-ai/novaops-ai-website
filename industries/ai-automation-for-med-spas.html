Our system is designed to avoid collecting or storing protected health information (PHI) in the CRM or AI conversation flows. Appointment booking and follow-up communications are structured to stay on the administrative side of HIPAA — scheduling, confirmations, reactivation offers. Any clinical information (medical history, consent forms, treatment notes) remains in your HIPAA-compliant EHR or EMR system, which we do not touch. We recommend consulting with your compliance counsel for your specific situation, and we're happy to document our data handling practices.
