Insurance Agency AI Automation Questions

We build the AI with a clear boundary between answering general coverage questions (which it does well) and providing specific coverage advice (which requires a licensed agent). When a conversation reaches a threshold where licensed advice is needed, the AI escalates immediately to a human agent. The AI handles qualification, objection handling at the information level, scheduling, and follow-up — it never provides binding quotes or specific coverage recommendations. This keeps it well within appropriate use for insurance-regulated communication.

Yes — all SMS communications are sent only to leads who have provided their phone number and opted in through your website, web forms, or direct contact. We build opt-in confirmation into the initial AI conversation, and every sequence includes a clear opt-out mechanism ("Reply STOP to unsubscribe"). We recommend working with your compliance team to ensure your web forms include proper consent language, which we can help you draft. We do not purchase or contact any third-party lead lists.

This keeps conversations relevant and the qualification data meaningful for your agents.

Your GoHighLevel CRM pipeline tracks every lead from initial quote request through qualification, agent handoff, and close.
