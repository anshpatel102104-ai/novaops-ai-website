We connect your lead sources to GoHighLevel via native integrations or Zapier. The same works for Realtor.com, Facebook Lead Ads, BoldLeads, Market Leader, your website forms, and most other lead sources. If your lead source has a Zapier integration or can send an email notification, we can connect it.

Yes — we build completely separate qualification flows for buyers and sellers. Buyer qualification focuses on timeline, pre-approval status, price range, and target area. Seller qualification focuses on motivation to sell, timeline, property details, and whether they need to buy simultaneously. Both flows route to the appropriate agent on your team based on your routing rules (geography, specialization, availability, round-robin, etc.).

We build your routing logic into GoHighLevel. Common setups include: round-robin distribution, zip code-based routing (lead in a specific area goes to the agent who works that area), price-range-based routing, or seniority-based routing where top producers get first right on the best leads. You decide the rules — we implement them. Agents receive an SMS notification with the lead's name, qualification details, and a direct link to the conversation in GHL when a hot lead is assigned to them.

We build your automation system inside GoHighLevel, which becomes your primary AI and communication layer. If you're currently using Follow Up Boss, LionDesk, Sierra Interactive, or another real estate CRM, we can often set up a two-way sync via Zapier so both systems stay updated. Many teams use GHL as their AI automation engine and keep their existing CRM for transaction management. For teams willing to consolidate, GHL handles both pipeline and transaction workflows with some configuration.
