The Process

From Cold Lead to Booked Client — Automatically

Five steps that run in the background while you focus on the work.

01

Lead Enters the System

A new lead arrives from any source — web form, inbound call, paid ad, or manually imported. Their contact details and context are logged in your CRM automatically.

02

Sequence Starts

The AI selects the right follow-up sequence for this lead type and begins the 90-day program. Day 1 message fires within minutes of contact — while the lead is still warm.

03

Personalized Messages Delivered

Each message is personalized with the lead's name, their specific inquiry, and stage-appropriate content. Messages rotate between SMS and email to maximize open rates and stay top-of-mind.

04

Lead Replies

When the lead engages — at Day 1, Day 30, or Day 88 — the sequence stops instantly. No more robotic messages to someone who's already interested. The AI pivots to a real conversation.
