The Process

From Missed Call to Booked Job in Under 10 Minutes

Four steps that recover leads your competitors would have claimed — automatically, around the clock.

01

Missed Call Detected

The moment a call goes unanswered — whether you're on another call, on a job, or it's 2 AM — the system detects it within seconds. No manual monitoring required. No calls to check.

02

SMS Fires Within 20 Seconds

A personalized, human-sounding text fires to the caller's number within 20 seconds. It's sent from a local area code number, uses your business name, and opens with a friendly, natural greeting.

03

AI Takes the Conversation

When the caller replies, the AI takes over — asking what they need, qualifying their request, and assessing urgency. The conversation feels natural and moves quickly toward a resolution or booking.

04

Lead Qualifies and Books

Qualified leads are sent a booking link or transferred to dispatch. Warm leads who aren't ready enter a multi-touch SMS follow-up sequence. Every outcome is tracked and reported in your monthly summary.
