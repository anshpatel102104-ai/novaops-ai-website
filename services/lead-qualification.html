How It Qualifies

Conversational AI That Screens Every Lead Before They Reach You

The AI starts a natural conversation the moment a lead contacts you — via SMS, chat, or form submission. It asks 2–3 business-specific qualifying questions tailored to what actually matters for your service: timeline, budget, property ownership, case type, or whatever criteria you define.

Based on the answers, every lead gets scored and classified as Hot, Warm, or Cold. Hot leads are routed to your team immediately. Warm leads enter a nurture sequence. Cold leads are tracked — but your team's time is protected.

  • Natural conversation — not a form or interrogation
  • 2–3 custom qualifying questions per business type
  • Instant Hot / Warm / Cold classification
  • Hot leads routed to your team with context summary
  • Warm leads enter automated follow-up sequence
  • 94% accuracy — validated across industries

The Process

From Inbound Contact to Qualified Appointment

A seamless five-step flow that turns raw inquiries into actionable, scored leads — without any human involvement.

01

Lead Contacts Your Business

A prospect reaches out via any channel — inbound SMS, web chat widget, contact form, or Facebook lead ad. The AI intercepts the conversation immediately, before any human needs to respond.

02

AI Starts a Conversation

The AI responds in a warm, natural tone — branded to your business. It acknowledges their inquiry and transitions naturally into the qualifying conversation, with no robotic scripting or impersonal surveys.

03

Asks Qualifying Questions

2–3 targeted questions surface the key decision criteria for your service — ownership status, budget, timeline, location, case type, or whatever matters most. Questions are customized specifically for your business during onboarding.

04

Scores the Lead

Answers are analyzed against your qualification criteria. The lead receives a score from 0–100 and a Hot / Warm / Cold label — with a brief summary of why they were scored that way.

Use Cases

The Right Questions for Every Industry

Qualification criteria are fully customized — what qualifies a solar lead is completely different from a law firm intake.

☀️

Solar Companies

The two questions that define a solar lead: do they own their home, and what's their electric bill? AI screens these instantly — so your sales reps only call qualified homeowners with real savings potential.

⚖️

Law Firms

AI screens case type, jurisdiction, incident timeline, and injury severity before routing to intake. Attorneys only spend time on viable cases — dramatically improving conversion from consult to retained client.

✨

Med Spas

Match every inquiry to the right service offering. The AI asks about treatment goals, skin type, previous procedures, and budget — then routes to the appropriate provider and sends a relevant service menu.
