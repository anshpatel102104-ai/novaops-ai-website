The Process

From Ringing Phone to Confirmed Appointment

Five seamless steps that handle every inbound call without you touching your phone.

01

Call Comes In

Any inbound call to your business number — day or night, weekday or weekend — is answered by the Voice AI within 2 rings. No voicemail, no hold music, no missed opportunity.

02

Voice AI Answers

The agent answers using your business name in a natural, friendly voice. It identifies itself as your business assistant — not a robot — and opens the conversation professionally, the same way every time.

03

Qualifies the Caller

Through natural back-and-forth conversation, the agent surfaces what the caller needs, their timeline, and whether they're a fit for your services. No scripts — the AI adapts to whatever the caller says.

04

Books or Escalates

If the caller is qualified and ready, the AI books directly into your live calendar and confirms the appointment verbally. If the call requires a human — complex requests, upset callers, or high-value opportunities — it escalates instantly with a full summary.
