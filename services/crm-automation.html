The Process

From Raw Lead to Full Pipeline Intelligence

Four automated steps that give you complete visibility without any admin overhead.

01

Lead Enters the System

Any new lead — from any source — is automatically captured and a contact record is created in GoHighLevel. Name, phone, email, source, and first-contact timestamp all logged without anyone touching a keyboard.

02

CRM Auto-Creates Contact

The contact is built with full context: source attribution, campaign data, inquiry type, and initial qualification score. Custom fields are populated based on how the lead came in and what they said.

03

AI Scores & Tags

The AI analyzes response speed, message content, qualifying criteria, and behavioral signals to score the lead 0–100. Hot leads (70+) get an immediate team alert. Warm leads enter a follow-up sequence. Cold leads are nurtured long-term.

04

Monthly Report Delivered

On the 1st of every month, a comprehensive performance report is emailed directly to you: total leads, conversion rates, revenue attributed, pipeline velocity, and top-performing lead sources — automatically generated.
