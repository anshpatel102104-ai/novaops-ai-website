How It Works

The Process

From Lead to Booked Appointment in Minutes

Four automated steps run in the background so you never have to chase a prospect again.

01

Lead Qualifies

A prospect contacts your business — via web form, SMS, or ad. Our AI asks 2–3 qualifying questions to confirm they're a genuine fit before any booking action is taken.

02

AI Sends Booking Link

The moment qualification is confirmed, the AI delivers a personalized booking link via SMS. The link connects directly to your live calendar — no back-and-forth scheduling calls needed.

03

Confirmation + Reminders

Once booked, an instant confirmation fires via SMS and email. A 24-hour reminder and a 2-hour reminder follow automatically — reducing no-shows by up to 50% without any manual effort.

04

No-Show Recovery

If a prospect doesn't show, a recovery sequence fires within minutes. Most no-shows get rebooked within 24 hours — turning a lost slot into a second chance at revenue.
