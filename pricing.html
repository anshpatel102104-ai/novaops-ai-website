FAQ

Common Pricing Questions

Is there a contract or lock-in?

The Rev-Share plan runs month-to-month after the first 30 days. The Done-For-You plan is a one-time engagement — you own the system with no ongoing commitment. The Monthly Retainer requires 60 days notice to cancel. No long-term lock-ins.

What if I don’t get the 10 appointments in 30 days?

We continue working at zero charge until you do. This isn’t a marketing promise — it’s in our service agreement. If the system doesn’t perform, we optimize, rebuild, or pivot at our cost, not yours.

What does “revenue generated” mean in the Rev-Share plan?

We attribute revenue to the AI system using tracking tags on leads, appointments booked through the system, and deal close data from your CRM. We work with you to agree on the attribution model before launch so there are no disputes.

Are there any platform or tool costs on top of these prices?

GoHighLevel runs around $97–$297/month depending on your plan. Twilio SMS costs are typically $20–$80/month based on volume. We help you set these up at the lowest appropriate tier. All costs are disclosed upfront — no hidden fees.

Can I upgrade from one plan to another?

Yes. Many clients start on Rev-Share to validate results, then move to Done-For-You or Monthly Retainer as revenue grows. We credit the setup fee against the build cost when you upgrade.

Do you offer custom pricing for agencies or multi-location businesses?

Yes. For businesses with multiple locations, franchises, or agencies wanting to resell our systems, we have custom volume pricing. Book a strategy call to discuss your specific situation.
